What it handles

Designed for real pharmacy workflows with visible AI support

AI Based Pharmacy System connects supplier and chemist operations while using historical bill data to forecast demand and recommend stock levels.

AI Smart Stock

Supplier can maintain stock while the system highlights predicted demand, suggested stock, low-stock alerts, and reorder quantity.

Chemist Ordering

Chemist can place stock requests, select supplier, choose payment timing, view order status, and track supplier response.

Billing And Payment

Accepted orders can be converted into professional invoices with pending/paid payment status visible to both roles.

AI Demand Forecasting

Historical generated bills are used for monthly trend prediction, confidence scoring, and actionable stock recommendations.
